Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI gym finder work?
Our AI chatbot asks you questions about your fitness goals, location preferences, and workout style. It then analyzes hundreds of gyms across Thailand to recommend the perfect matches for you in under 60 seconds.
Are the gym videos and virtual tours free to access?
Yes! All gym videos, virtual tours, and basic information are completely free. You only pay when you're ready to book a membership or trial session at your chosen gym.
Can I visit multiple gyms before choosing one?
Absolutely! Many of our partner gyms offer free trial sessions or day passes. The AI can help you schedule visits to your top matches so you can experience each gym before committing.
What cities in Thailand do you cover?
We currently cover Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket, Pattaya, and Koh Samui, with over 500+ verified gym locations. We're constantly expanding to new cities based on demand.
Do you work with Muay Thai gyms and specialized training facilities?
Yes! Our database includes traditional gyms, CrossFit boxes, Muay Thai gyms, yoga studios, climbing gyms, and specialized training facilities. Just tell the AI what type of training you're looking for.
How much does it cost to use the gym finder?
The AI gym finder and all gym information are free to use. You only pay membership fees directly to the gym you choose. We don't charge any finder's fees or commissions.
